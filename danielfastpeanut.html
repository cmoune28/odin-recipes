Daniel Fast Peanut Butter Balls

Description

Healthy, not too sweet, a great snack for anytime! My girls, 2 and 4, love them!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown rice syrip
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups oats, or more to taste
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened flaked coconut

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine brown rice syrup and coconut oil together in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir melted and boiling, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat.

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix peanut butter and vanilla extract into syrup mixture until smooth. Add oats, raisins, and coconut to peanut butter mixture and mix well. Refrigerate mixture until cool, at least 30 minutes. Form mixture into balls and freeze until set, at least 1 hour.
